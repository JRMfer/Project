About this dashboard

A current intersting topic is the devlopement oft the total transfer fees in football. This amount has even a record value in 2018 ( link ). This dashboard tries to visualisze this devlopment and some indicators.

The visualizations

World map

The world map shows the total transfers fees and amounts of transfers for a certain time period and/or position. Hovering over a country shows the exact amounts in that particular country. Clicking a country updates the other three visualizations according the country selected. The same result can be achieved by selecting the country in the dropdown menu.

Line chart

By clicking the button in the line chart one sees the development of the total transfer fees over time. Once a selection is made either by one of the dropdown menus or by clicking on one of the other 3 visualizations the line chart will be updated and one can see the "adjusted" development over time. While hovering over the points a tooltip arrives showing the total transfer fees and the top 3 transfers for in the corresponding season. All together one not only sees the devlopment of the total transfer fees over time but also that of the 3 highest amounts.

Bar chart

The bar chart shows the distribution of the total transfer fees either by country or by club of a certain country and therefore tries to explain which country/club have a bigger part in the total transfer fees. While hovering one sees the total amount of transfers and transfer fees per category. If a country is clicked then the sunburst, line graph and the bar chart itself will be updated and if a club is clicked then only the sunburst will be updated.

Sunburst

The sunburst shows the distriution of the total transfer fees in the world according the season and/or position requested by the dropdown menus. While hovering one sees the total transfer fees of the corresponding country or club and it's weight in the total transfer fees for respectively the world and the corresponding country. If a country or club is clicked then the sunbusrt, bar chart and line chart will be updated. With this visualization one can get a more in depth understanding of the distribution of the transfer fees.

Some Remarks

  • Altough the dataset is offered at the Kaggle database it is not sure if the dataset is 100% correct. I've already encountered that some clubs are stored in the wrong competition (see China).
  • Please note that only the transfer fees are considered and additional bonuses or other possible incomes are neglected.
  • Last remark is that the dataset has been adjusted for the purpose of this project: All competition names are replaced by the corresponding country names.
